Overview

The Jadeon are masters of elemental magic, specializing in ice and thunder spells that can devastate both single targets and groups of enemies. With their elegant swords that channel magical energy, Jadeon strike a balance between raw power and tactical versatility.

Strengths

  • Excellent AoE damage capabilities
  • Strong crowd control with freezing effects
  • Flexible playstyle adaptable to different situations
  • Good mobility for a spellcaster
  • Flashy and visually impressive skills

Weaknesses

  • Relatively squishy with low defense
  • Mana management can be challenging
  • Some skills have long cast times
  • Requires good positioning to maximize effectiveness

Affinity Tips

Affinities provide powerful bonuses and abilities that can significantly enhance your Jadeon's capabilities.

Felkin

★★★★★
One shot, High DPS

Perfect for maximizing damage output. Felkin's burst damage abilities work exceptionally well with Jadeon's thunder skills.

Best Skill(s):

  • Magnificient Spirit II/ Essence Flash II - Massive single-target damage
Best for: Raids, Boss Fights, PvE Content

Dagos

★★★★★
Crowd Control, Skill Evasive

Excellent for PvP situations where control and evasion are crucial. The crowd control abilities complement Jadeon's freezing skills.

Best Skill(s):

  • Harsh Reality II - Debuff and Fasting
Best for: PvP, Arena, World PvP

Fuwa

★★★★★
Tank, Skill Interrupt

Useful for solo content where survivability is important. Provides interrupts that can stop dangerous enemy abilities.

Best Skill(s):

  • Nature Mystery II - Reliable interrupt and SP drain
Best for: Solo Play, Survival, Difficult Content
