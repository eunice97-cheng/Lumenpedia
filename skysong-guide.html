Overview

The Skysong are mystical Spellcasters who use their abilities to support their allies and hinder their enemies. They can turn the tide of battle through powerful buffs, healing, and crowd control.

Strengths

  • Excellent party-wide buffs and auras
  • Strong healing and regeneration abilities
  • Versatile crowd control skills
  • Good ressistances against most ailment status
  • High utility in group content

Weaknesses

  • Low personal damage output
  • Vulnerable when focused by enemies
  • Low Mobility
  • Complex skill rotations for optimal play
  • Dependent on party members for protection

Affinity Tips

Affinities provide powerful bonuses and abilities that can significantly enhance your Skysong's capabilities.

Felkin

★★★★☆
DoT, Max HP Debuff

Not ideal for support-focused Skysong, but can provide some real damage by combo skill

Best Skill(s):

  • High Aspiration II - HP and Skill Damage Debuff
Best for: Hybrid Support/Debuff

Dagos

★★★★★
Crowd Control

Excellent for PvP support. Dagos' crowd control help Skysong survive while supporting the team.

Best Skill(s):

  • The Four Elements II - Perfect skill for Immeasurable Truth combo
Best for: PvP Support, Arena

Fuwa

★★★★★
Tank, Healing, Buffer

Good for survivability in difficult content. Healing and Buffs support help protect the party from dangerous abilities.

Best Skill(s):

  • Spiritual Exchange II - Party protection
Best for: Raid and PvP Support, Difficult PvE
