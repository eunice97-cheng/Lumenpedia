Overview

The Lupin are agile assassins who strike at high speed, specializing in high critical dmage. With their glaive and phantom-like agility, Lupin can quickly eliminate key targets.

Strengths

  • Extremely high burst damage
  • Excellent mobility and evasion
  • Strong single-target elimination
  • Powerful Silence effect
  • Fast attack speed

Weaknesses

  • Very low defense and HP
  • Vulnerable to area-of-effect attacks
  • Complex positioning requirements
  • Lack of AoE offensive ability
  • Higly disadvantages against long ranged attacks

Affinity Tips

Affinities provide powerful bonuses and abilities that can significantly enhance your Lupin's capabilities.

Felkin

★★★★★
Fasting, High DPS

Perfect for maximizing burst damage. Felkin's damage amplification works exceptionally well with Lupin's assassination skills.

Best Skill(s):

  • Mountain Song II - Incredible fasting effect for PvP
Best for: PvP, Burst Assassination

Dagos

★★★★★
Damage burst, HP Regeneration

Excellent for both PvE and PvP. Good damage and great skill evasion.

Best Skill(s):

  • River Dweller II - Dealing damages based on HP
Best for: PvP and PvE with overall balance

Fuwa

★★★★☆
Tank, Debuffer, Self Buffing

Less ideal for Lupin's playstyle, but can provide some utility in specific situations.

Best Skill(s):

  • Lovesickness II - Skills reset, CD reduction
Best for: Extreme PvP players
