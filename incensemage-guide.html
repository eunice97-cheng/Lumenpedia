Overview

The Incense Mages are mystical spellcasters who harness the power of sacred incense to manipulate the battlefield. With their aromatic spells and smoke-based magic, they can deal damage while providing unique support effects to their allies.

Strengths

  • Unique damage-over-time effects
  • Powerful area denial capabilities
  • Supportive buffs and debuffs
  • Good crowd control options
  • Flexible role in parties

Weaknesses

  • Complex skill combinations
  • Setup time required for optimal damage
  • Vulnerable to interrupt effects
  • Mana-intensive playstyle
