Understanding the Lumen Interface

Learn about the key features and functionality of the Lumen game website

Key Features Overview

The Lumen website interface is designed to provide easy access to all game features, account management, and community tools. Here's what you need to know:

Navigation System

The interface is divided into a few display and navigation areas:

  • Top Bar: Mainly a display bar showing how much of each currencies you are carrying, account ID, VIP Level with some additional language and setting change buttons
  • Main Menu: Core game sections and features but mostly for viewing
  • Navigation Menu: Additional services and shops etc
  • Center Menu: Mostly are display menu
  • Side Social Media Menu: For Navigation to the Social Media pages
  • Side Navigation Menu: Important : Give some extra attention to Quests and Events

Currency System

Lumen uses multiple currencies for different purposes:

  • Lumen: Earned through gameplay and voting
  • Web Gold: Direct conversion from in-game gold. They are basically what you earn from in-game
  • Blood Lumen: Premium currency for special items. Also equilvalent to Jaden in-game
  • PvP Token: Another premium currency for special items. Only obtainable from attending PvP events.

Shop and Trade System

Lumen uses multiple shop and trade system:

  • Shop: Free items which require only Lumen, web gold or PvP Token
  • Divine Trader: Exotic items which require only web gold
  • Market: Unlike Banker Etsuk (in-game), there is no commission charges for trading Blood Lumen and gold here
Priority

Getting Started

New players should focus on these key areas first:

Quests on Side Navigation

  • Complete the voting system every 12 hours for free Lumen
  • Spin the Daily Wheel for rewards
  • Claim your Daily Devotion rewards
  • Claim your Battle Pass rewards (if there are any)
  • Check for any ongoing and upcoming PvP Events
  • Check for any ongoing and upcoming PvE Events

Check these Daily

  • Visit the Divine Trader to check for any good deals
  • Visit the Rewards page to check if you are entitled to any rewards based on your current progression
  • Visit the Stash page to check list of items which are available to be send to your character in-game
Optional

Exclusive for VIP

Becoming a VIP of the server :

Donation Page

  • Choose the Offering (Amount)
  • Reward Options : Blood Lumen (Website) or Jaden (In-Game)
  • Category of Offering : Crypto (for 30% extra Celestial Blesssing) or Paypal or Divine Card

Donation Features

  • Receive Blood Lumen or Jaden for premium items
  • Access to Rewards - Donation
  • Special donator ranks and discord titles
  • Able to give the server blessings through Sponsor page
  • Support server maintenance and development
Editor Notes

Just a Share of Thoughts

Insights and Reminders from the editor

Insights

Both as a player and an editor, I've fielded numerous inquiries of this nature.

  • Isn't this a free-to-play server? Why are they taking donations in exchange for Blood Lumen/Jaden and VIP rewards?

    • Maintaining a stable server requires significant investment in hardware, hosting, bandwidth, and DDoS protection.

  • Providing donators access to Jaden Mall and Premium Rewards—isn't that considered a pay-to-win server?

    • The topic of donations is a perennial debate among players. Voluntary contributions from those who simply wish to show gratitude are often insufficient to cover server costs. To secure the necessary funding, in-game incentives become essential. However, I do believe that Lumen server(s) enforces strict policies to ensure these premium rewards remain within a generally acceptable range and will not cross into game-breaking territory.

    Reminders

    • Donation / VIP is optional
    • This support is what keeps our world online and running smoothly
    • Premium items are available to all players. While VIPs get instant access, others can earn them through dedicated gameplay
    • Not everyone can contribute financially and that's okay! There are still other meaningful ways to show your support

      • - Promoting the server and inviting friends.

      - Being a positive and helpful member in the community.

      - Reporting bugs and providing constructive feedback.

      - Creating fan art, videos, or guides. (Join me in Lumenpedia for example)

    • Always remember that donations are just one way to help. Your time and positivity are equally valuable to our community.
